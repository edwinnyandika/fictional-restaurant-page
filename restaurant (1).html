Our Story

A Passion for Honest Cooking

Ember & Oak was born from a simple conviction: that the best food is honest food. Founded in 2014 by Chef Miriam Sorel, our kitchen draws on wood-fire techniques passed down through generations, married with the finest seasonal produce from local farms within a 50-mile radius.

Every dish on our menu tells a story — of soil and season, of tradition and curiosity. We believe dining should be an unhurried ritual, not a transaction.
